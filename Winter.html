WINTER

Japan

Japan is a beautiful country to visit year-round, but winter is an extraordinary time. The snow-capped mountains, sparkling city lights, and festive atmosphere make it a magical place to experience the holiday season.

Hokkaido is gorgeous all year round, and many people love exploring its national parks during summer, but the island is especially known for its winter scenery.

Ashikaga Flower Park in Tochigi Prefecture holds one of the largest light festivals in the country from October to February.

Shirakawa-go and Gokayama Gassho Zukuri Houses which are the ancient farmhouses of Gifu and Toyama Prefectures are need on your list of must visit places in Japan.

The Jigokudani Monkey Park maintains natural hot springs that attract wild monkeys to warm up in during winter.

Korea

One of the best times of the year to visit South Korea is in the winter. When the snow blankets the city, the whole town becomes a mini winter wonderland.

The Jisan Ski Resort is the perfect getaway for winter in Korea. Whether you’re looking to spend a day shredding down the slopes or just looking to explore the area, we’ve got you covered.

Head to Oneday Hanbok rental in the Jung-gu district of Seoul, where a team of friendly staff is waiting to assist you. Choose from various designs and accessories to find the best look that suits your style.

Visit Nami Island in winter with its tree-lined avenues covered in white snow. Stroll along the lanes of trees hand-in-hand with partner and recreate some famous Korean “Winter Sonata” drama scenes.

Discover more attractions in South Korea when winter, such as the Pohang Roller Coaster Space Walk. it found in Hwanho Park, it has a great view of Pohang City and Yeongildae Beach in the distance.

Thailand

Winter is the best climate to visit Thailand. The air is less humid and the sun doesn’t shine down so harshly that you are drenched in sweat within minutes. Evenings are pleasant with a cool breeze blowing in from the sea.

During December to February, the whole space of Northern Hemisphere, and the city of Chiang Mai, replete with pink wild cherry blossoms. So, if you visit this city during winter you will be amazed to see the sunflower fields in full bloom.

Thailand is tropical country so, during winter is the ideal time to reach the Bangpu Recreation Center in Samut Prakan, where the tourists can get ample scope to watch the group of migratory birds.

Muen-Ngoen Road Tri Trang Beach is what you always dreamt of calm and tranquil, delved into the serene abundance. Strolling by the beach or just laying back and relax is the best thing that you could do.

There are quite some places to visit in winter in Thailand like Doi Inthanon in Chiang Mai and Mae Ya Waterfall, which offer significant hiking opportunities.

Malaysia

Some winter holiday travelers prefer to enjoy their time in warm climate without worrying too much about cold winters. Malaysia is one of the most ideal winter getaways to escape from thick snow and freezing winds.

Escape from the thick snow and chilly weather to enjoy the sunny skies, sandy beaches and sea breezes at Malaysia’s tropical islands such as Pangkor Island and Perhentian Island.

Perhentian Island is one of the ideal places to explore Malaysia’s beautiful coral reefs. With its tropical weather and warm seas, you don’t have to worry about freezing while diving into seas.

Explore the rain forest’s wildlife at Bako National Park and Kinabalu Park. You might meet some animal friends during your exploration.

Challenge yourself and discover Malaysia’s limestone caves. Cave and river tours are provided to assist you during exploration such as Gua tempurung, it has three large chambers with amazing rock formations.

China

As winter approaches, it’s time to embrace the amazing snow and ice wonders in the north or savor forever warm in the south in China. Happy Chinese New Year also comes. Such grand and traditional festival will add more authenticity to a China vacation.

Harbin the Ice City in China, belongs to one of the best winter getaways for world friends globally famous for its spectacular annual World Ice and Snow Festival gala during winter.

Huangshan Mountain (Yellow Mountain), is widely believed as the most beautiful mountain in China. It features the “Four Wonders” beautifully-shaped pines trees, fantastic rock peaks, sea of clouds and hot springs.

Mount Emei near Chengdu, China is the mountain combines authentic Buddhist culture and diverse nature with its up to 28 temples and monasteries that dot in the tranquil mountain sides from foot to the summit.

Xiling Snow Mountain boasting the largest-scaled alpine ski resort (Xiling Ski Resort) in whole Southern China is the joyous “Winter Wonderland” if you want to have fun in the snow world in the Southern region.

Indonesia

Indonesia is the asia Indonesia has a tropical climate. Hence, there is no winter in Indonesia in the strictest sense, but one could consider the wet period, between December and March, as winter.

Bali tour should be on your list. This island is known all over the world for its stunning landscapes, temples, and monuments.

Raja Ampat is one of the most stunning archipelagoes in Indonesia. Home to plenty of charming islands, this place is ideal for those wanting to see local wildlife.

The capital of Indonesia which is Jakarta, is the ideal spot for those wanting to explore the urban culture, nightlife and tourist attractions.

Surabaya and Bromo National Park. The main city of the eastern part of Java, Surabaya, offers to plunge into the local flavor and enjoy the atmosphere of the Asian historical center.
